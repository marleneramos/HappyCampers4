Read and select.

  1. He does chores every Friday.

    He does chores every week    every day    .

  2. She cleans her room when she gets up.

    She cleans her room every morning    every week    .

  3. We go to bed early every day.

    We never    always     stay up late.

  4. They don’t exercise every day.

    They always    sometimes     exercise.

  5. We always study for tests.

    We study for tests every day    sometimes    .
